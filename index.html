Vortex Deployment Decoder

Name the nameless mods
PGPatcher won't.

PGPatcher shows some mods as garbled codes like 99_33_27_993327ba… instead of their real names. This reads your vortex.deployment.json and works out what each coded mod actually is — so you can match them to your PGPatcher screen and know which ones a reinstall would fix, and which to leave alone.

1

Find your file

Locate vortex.deployment.json — see the note just below.

2

Drop it in

Drag the file onto the panel below. It's read on your device — nothing is uploaded.

3

Match your screen

Compare the list to the codes actually showing in your PGPatcher. Green ones showing a code can be reinstalled to fix the name.

◈

Where is the file? It lives in your Skyrim Data folder: …\Skyrim Special Edition\Data\vortex.deployment.json. Quickest route: in Vortex, right-click the Skyrim game entry → Open Game Folder, then open the Data folder and grab vortex.deployment.json.
